# [AI Engineer Career Path](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/plans/odgoumq07e4x83#learn-wwl-implement-knowledge-mining-azure-cognitive-search)

Artificial intelligence (AI) engineers are responsible for developing, programming and training the complex networks of algorithms that make up AI so that they can function like a human brain. This role requires combined expertise in **software development, programming, data science and data engineering**.

## Milestone 1 - Azure AI Engineer Associate self-paced training

### Learning Path
- [Get started with Azure AI Services](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/get-started-azure-ai/)
- [Develop computer vision solutions in Azure](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/create-computer-vision-solutions-azure-ai/)
- [Develop natural language solutions in Azure](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/develop-language-solutions-azure-ai/)
- [Implement knowledge mining with Azure AI Search](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/implement-knowledge-mining-azure-cognitive-search/)
- [Develop solutions with Azure AI Document Intelligences](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/extract-data-from-forms-document-intelligence/)
- [Develop Generative AI solutions with Azure OpenAI in Foundry Models](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/develop-ai-solutions-azure-openai/)
